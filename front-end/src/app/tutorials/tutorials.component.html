- Youtubers We Love -


Brandon James Greer Brandon James Greer is a YouTuber who publishes weekly pixel art timelapses. Each video is narrated, with the host detailing his work flow and thought process. These are fun to watch and great sources of inspiration.

Adam C. Younis Adam C. Younis is a YouTuber and Twitch streamer who specializes in pixel art and animation. He has been a full time indie game developer for several years and supports himself with his game development content on Twitch. His videos are good sources of technical information for more advanced pixel art techniques.
